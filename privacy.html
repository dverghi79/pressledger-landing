  • Objection: object to processing based on legitimate interest

To exercise these rights, email info@leanaistudio.com with "CCPA Request" in the subject line. We will verify your identity and respond within 45 days.

Categories of personal information collected in the preceding 12 months: identifiers (email, name), commercial information (revenue range), and internet activity (browsing data via analytics). We do not sell personal information to third parties.
